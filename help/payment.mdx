# Billing

<Accordion title="How do I change my billing details?">

To update your billing details:

1. Go to the **Billing Page** in your dashboard.
   ![Billing Page](/images/manage-subscription.png)
2. Click the "Manage Subscription" button.
3. Select the subscription you want to update and click "Update Payment Method."
   ![Update payment method](/images/billing-info-2.png)

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I view my invoice history?">

To view your invoices history:

1. Go to the **Billing Page** in your dashboard and click the "Manage Subscription" button (this button will only appear if you have a subscription).
   ![Billing Page](/images/manage-subscription.png)
2. Scroll down to the "Billing History" section to view all your invoices.
   ![Billing History](/images/billing-history.png)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">

If you run out of credits before the next billing cycle, you can either:

- Wait for the next cycle to start, or
- Renew your subscription early by clicking the "Renew Subscription" button on the billing page.
  ![Renew subscription](/images/renew-subscription.png)

After you provide your payment details, the billing cycle will be reset and will continue from the day you renewed your subscription. And you will be assigned the number of credits in your plan.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cancel a plan?">

To cancel a plan:

1. Navigate to the **Billing Page**.
2. Click the "Unsubscribe" button.
   ![Unsubscribe](/images/unsubscribe.png)
3. Confirm the cancellation by clicking the "Confirm" button in the modal.
   ![Unsubscribe Modal](/images/unsubscribe-modal.png)

After cancellation:

- You will still have access to the remaining credits in your plan until the billing cycle ends.
- These credits will not carry over to the next subscription.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does my plan automatically renew?">

Yes, your plan will automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle.

</Accordion>
